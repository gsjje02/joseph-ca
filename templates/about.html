About this PKI Certificate Tool

You can use this tool to create root and intermediate certificate authority key pairs and certs. Then you can create end-entity certs signed and issued by your certificate authority(s). Types of end-entity certs include server/client, code-signing, user certs for people, and user-like certs for services AKA Non-Person Entity (NPE) certs. You can also use this tool to revoke certificates and generate certificate revocation lists (CRLs). You can also spawn off an OSCP listener for your CA(s).
